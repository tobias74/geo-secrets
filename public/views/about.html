geosecrets.com is a geo-based message-board. Your messages are only visible within the radius you choose. Changing the visibility-radius does not affect previously posted messages.

The visibility-radius also limits the messages you receive: Only those messages within your observed visibility-radius will be displayed, given that you are also within the visibility-radius defined by the sender

Additionally you can protect your messages by using secret channels.
